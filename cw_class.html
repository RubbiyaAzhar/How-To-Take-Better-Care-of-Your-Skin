Essentials of Skincare

Cleansing You can buy all the expensive skincare you want but if you are not getting under the skin to remove all the dirt and makeup then sorry honey you wont be able to reach clear skin anytime soon. But dont worry! Get cleansing and those pimples will disappear day by day:)

Exfoliating Your skin renews every 28 days and so its essential that you scrub off all the dead skin cells to get the secret to glowing skin, otherwise your skin will always look dull and tired! Exfoliating also smoothens the skin and applying makeup is a breeze!

Moisturizing Moisture is important for the skin! Your skin literally eats everything on it and moisturizer is the holy grail to prevent wrinking, tightning the skin, and prevetning dryness. For my oily sin girls out there, we actually need to moistrize a lot more, because when we do our skin will produce less oil as a skin protection barrier.

Sunscreen If you leave the house without suncreen, You my girl are literaly asking for your skin to age faster! UV rays are everywhere! Sunny or cloudy, UV rays will leave you with wrinkles at an early age. Never. Leave. The. House. Without. Sunscreen.
